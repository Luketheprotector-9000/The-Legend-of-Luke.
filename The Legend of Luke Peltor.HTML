The Unstoppable Luke Peltor

There are many legends, but none quite as captivating as Luke Peltor, the fierce Protector of the North Realm. Known for his unmatched charisma and jawline sharp enough to split diamonds, Luke has captivated hearts and minds across the land.

But it’s not just his appearance that has earned him his legendary status. Luke is currently on an epic 9000-day mewing streak—a feat unmatched by any mortal. Alongside him in this quest is his equally determined companion, Tom Smeethe, a fellow champion of posture and dedication.

The Mewing Streak

For 9000 days straight, Luke and Tom have demonstrated unwavering commitment to their mewing practice. The results? A jawline so sculpted it could command armies, and a bond so strong it’s forged in the fires of willpower.

The Strange Sounds of the Wild

On rare occasions, when the moon is high and the winds are right, you might hear something... strange. Tom and Luke, found together in the wild, make eerie moaning sounds. Some say it’s the sound of the ancient mewing energy coursing through them; others claim it’s the secret chant of the Northern Guardians. Either way, it’s enough to send shivers down your spine.
